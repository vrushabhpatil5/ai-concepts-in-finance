# AI Concepts in Finance

This repository documents notes, examples, and small demonstrations
exploring how artificial intelligence and generative models are applied
across finance, valuation, and decision-making.

The focus is on **conceptual understanding**, practical relevance,
and critical thinking rather than heavy model building.

---

## Topics Covered
- Generative AI in finance and accounting
- AI-assisted valuation and forecasting
- Trust, explainability, and adoption challenges
- Automation vs judgement in financial decision-making
- Risks, limitations, and ethical considerations

---

## Structure
- `notes/` – concept explanations and reflections  
- `examples/` – small demos or pseudo-code  
- `papers/` – summaries of academic or industry articles  

---

## Disclaimer
This repository is for learning and exploration purposes only
and does not constitute financial or investment advice.
